## Decision: Discipline vs Motivation

### Context
Long-term projects and skill development required consistent effort beyond short bursts of motivation.

### Options Considered
- Relying on motivation and interest
- Building discipline through routine and structure

### Decision
Chose discipline over motivation.

### Reasoning
- Motivation is inconsistent and external
- Discipline creates progress even on low-energy days
- Consistency compounds faster than intensity

### Trade-offs
- Can feel monotonous at times
- Requires upfront effort to build habits

### Outcome
Enabled steady progress in learning, project completion, and long-term skill development.
