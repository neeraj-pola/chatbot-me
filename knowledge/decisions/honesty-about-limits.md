## Decision: Being Honest About Limits

### Context
Technical interviews and team discussions often reward confidence, but exaggeration creates long-term issues.

### Options Considered
- Overselling skills and experience
- Being explicit about strengths and limits

### Decision
Chose honesty about limits.

### Reasoning
- Builds long-term trust
- Sets realistic expectations
- Encourages learning rather than defensiveness

### Trade-offs
- May appear less confident in short-term evaluations
- Requires comfort with uncertainty

### Outcome
Led to more constructive discussions and better alignment on responsibilities.
